Trade

An early form of trade, barter, saw the direct exchange of goods and services for other goods and services. Barter involves trading things without the use of money. Commerce

Some commentators trace the origins of commerce to the very start of transaction in prehistoric times. Apart from traditional self-sufficiency, trading became a principal facility of prehistoric people, who bartered what they had for goods and services from each other ( Barter system was popular in ancient times where one could get goods and services by offering the other person some other good and service according to their need instead of paying in monetary terms).
